About The Creators

Coney

Coney are a collection of makers creating interactive theatre, games and adventures where people can choose to take a meaningful part. Our mission is to spark change through play, always following the principles of adventure, curiosity, and loveliness. Our work takes place anywhere that people gather; in theatres, schools, museums, on the streets and online.

Everything we do is inspired by the belief that the world can be a magical place where ordinary people can do extraordinary things. Collaboration and dialogue are at the heart of our work: with audiences, peers, partners, and a network of artists.

The Golden Key has been created by Coney & Friends: we have worked with a vast network of partners, old and new, to put together this exciting programme, bringing together over 30 companies and 200 individual artists.

City of London

The Golden Key is the first major event commissioned by the City of London Corporation under Destination City, a new flagship programme aiming to enhance the Square Mile’s leisure offer with fun, inclusive and innovative events appealing to existing and new audiences.

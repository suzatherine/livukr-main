Travelling by River Bus

Take to the river with Uber Boat by Thames Clippers, London’s River Bus. Enjoy a comfortable seat, onboard café bar and fantastic views.

The weekend RB1 and RB2 services call at Tower Pier every 10-20 minutes and sail 9am-11pm, from Battersea Power Station in the west or Barking Riverside in the east, depending on the service.

Tickets to Tower Pier (Central Zone) start from £7.70 per adult, £3.85 per child (aged 5-15); kids 4 and under go free. The service is fully accessible to Tower Pier.

For more information visit thamesclippers.com.

Travelling by Train

  • It couldn’t be easier to visit the City by train with Southeastern, so you can sit back and relax as you travel into the heart of London. Southeastern offer a variety of ticket options and fares, plus there are no booking fees when you book direct with Southeastern.

    Advance

    Southeastern offer Advance tickets which are available on selected routes as single tickets at specific times and a great way to save on train travel. Book early for cheaper fares.

    Kids for a Quid

    An adult with a valid ticket can travel with up to 4 kids (aged 5-15) for just £1 each on off-peak Southeastern services, all year round. Under 5s go free.

    GroupSave

    Save up to 34% on the price of individual adult tickets when groups of 3-9 adults travel together off-peak. Plus, any kids travelling in addition to the group of adults will be eligible for Kids for a Quid tickets.
